# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Engineering leaders, delivery leads, security-minded operators, and project teams who need durable decision context across Slack, Jira, and GitHub.

## Product Purpose

Verachi creates cited decision records, owned risk records, and grounded answers from existing engineering workstreams. The landing page should make it clear what Verachi does, why the evidence model matters, and how teams can start.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, inspectable.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gloss, vague AI productivity claims, and pricing pages that hide the real action behind a manual pilot when self-serve signup exists.

## Design Principles

- Show evidence before claims.
- Keep trust and security visible in the main flow.
- Prefer clear account and rollout actions over vague sales language.
- Keep comparisons near the pricing decision.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast, keyboard-operable navigation and forms, reduced-motion support, semantic headings, and clear link text.
