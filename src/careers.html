How we work

Our engineering values.

Evidence first

Show your work

We cite our claims — in the product, in code reviews, and in internal discussions. Opinions are welcome. Unsupported claims are not. Every decision gets a record.

Controlled side effects

Measure twice

We move deliberately. Shipping fast matters, but shipping something that erodes trust matters more. We prefer conservative defaults and explicit opt-ins over "move fast and break things."

Craft over volume

Small team, high standards

We'd rather have fewer features that work beautifully than a bloated product nobody trusts. Code quality, design taste, and clear thinking are non-negotiable.
