How Verachi Works

The layer on top of your existing tools

No rip-and-replace. Verachi connects to Jira, Slack, and GitHub to observe, capture, and cite project activity.

1. Connect & Observe

Read-only integrations allow Verachi to observe real project activity without disrupting how developers and managers currently work.

2. Capture & Cite

Capture decisions with a simple emoji reaction (📌) or slash command. Verachi creates a summarized "Decision Record" with direct citations to the primary source.

3. Cascade & Reuse

Verachi instantly links and distributes decisions to the rest of the organization. When troubleshooting new incidents, similar past decisions are surfaced automatically.

Trust-First AI

"No citation, no claim." Every piece of AI-generated insight includes direct citations to the primary sources and a confidence indicator. External actions are approval-gated until you fully trust the system.
