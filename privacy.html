Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

What We Collect

We collect account details (name, email, organization), workspace configuration, and integration metadata needed to provide decision capture and search.

How We Use Data

We use your data to operate the product, secure your account, deliver support, and improve reliability. We do not sell personal data.

Integration Data

Verachi uses read-only scopes for supported integrations. We ingest references and context needed to index decisions, then link back to source systems.

Security

We apply encryption in transit, access controls, and tenant isolation to protect customer data. Access is limited to authorized personnel who need it to operate the service.

Retention

Data is retained while your workspace is active, then removed or anonymized according to your plan and contractual requirements.

Your Choices

You can request data export, correction, or deletion by contacting hello@verachi.io.
